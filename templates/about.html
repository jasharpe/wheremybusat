Where My Bus At?

This site uses publicly available transit schedule data to provide stop information for Grand River Transit, the transit provider for Kitchener-Waterloo. On the main page, it uses HTML5's geolocation APIs to find and display the schedules for the bus stops closest to you. This means instead of paying to text GRT and get stop times, or looking up large PDFs online, you can just go to wheremybusat.ca on your phone while standing at a bus stop, and instantly see all the upcoming scheduled arrivals at your stop.

The source code for this website is available on github.

This site contains information provided by the Regional Municipality of Waterloo under licence.
